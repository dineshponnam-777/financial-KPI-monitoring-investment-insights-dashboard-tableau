# 📊 Financial KPI Monitoring & Investment Insights Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **Financial KPI Monitoring & Investment Insights Dashboard** is an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to monitor and analyze key financial metrics, investment performance, profitability, portfolio value, and revenue trends.

This dashboard enables business leaders, financial analysts, investment managers, and decision-makers to gain actionable insights through intuitive visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to provide a centralized financial reporting solution that helps stakeholders:

✔ Monitor Revenue Performance

✔ Track Profitability Trends

✔ Evaluate Investment Returns (ROI)

✔ Analyze Portfolio Value

✔ Compare Budget vs Actual Revenue

✔ Identify High-Performing Regions

✔ Assess Investment Category Performance

✔ Support Data-Driven Financial Decisions

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 🌎 Revenue by Region

Analyzes revenue contribution across:

- Asia Pacific
- Middle East
- India
- North America
- Europe

### 💹 Profit by Investment Category

Evaluates profitability across:

- Fixed Income
- ETF
- Real Estate
- Mutual Fund
- Equity

### 🥧 Asset Type Distribution

Portfolio allocation across:

- Stocks
- Bonds
- ETF
- Gold
- Real Estate

### 🔥 ROI Heatmap

Visual comparison of ROI performance by:

- Region
- Investment Category

### 📈 Monthly Revenue vs Profit Trend Analysis

Tracks monthly performance trends and growth patterns throughout the year.

### 🎯 Investment vs ROI Analysis

Scatter plot used to identify relationships between:

- Investment Amount
- Return on Investment (ROI)

### 📊 Budget vs Actual Revenue

Compares:

- Budgeted Revenue
- Actual Revenue

to evaluate business performance and forecasting accuracy.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🏆 Revenue Performance

- Asia Pacific generated the highest revenue.
- Europe contributed the lowest revenue among analyzed regions.

### 💰 Profitability Analysis

- Fixed Income investments delivered the highest profit.
- Equity investments generated comparatively lower profit.

### 📈 ROI Analysis

- ROI performance varies significantly across regions and categories.
- Certain investment categories demonstrate superior return potential.

### 💼 Portfolio Diversification

- Portfolio allocation is diversified across multiple asset classes.
- Diversification helps reduce concentration risk.

### 📊 Revenue Trends

- Revenue and profit exhibit positive growth trends toward year-end.
- Financial performance remains relatively stable across periods.
